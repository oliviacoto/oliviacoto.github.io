[ABOUT ME]

Hello, my name is Olivia (some people call me Liv). I was born 19 years ago into a Cuban-American family in Southern California. I am now living in Miami, Florida studying at The University of Miami, while also soaking up my Cuban culture. My interests include fashion, makeup, music, and photography. I’m majoring in Public Relations with the hopes of working in the music or make-up industry. My blog was meant to be a representation of who I am, what I like, and how I feel.  As long as you stay tuned, I will keep you in the loop. Stay connected and watch for more fun things to happen in my life—enjoy the ride
