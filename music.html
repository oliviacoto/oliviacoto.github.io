BIRDS IN THE TRAP SING MCKNIGHT

Travis Scott, rapper and producer from Houston, released his sophomore album, Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight, on September 2, 2016. On September 11, 2016, the album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200, and the following day, Universal Music Group, announced that the label signed a worldwide deal with Scott. It starts with the tracks “the ends” and “way back”. They blend seamlessly to give the illusion of one song. André 3000 is also featured in “the ends”, which makes it 10x better. This brings me to the features on this album. Unreal. Some people shame on artists that have a lot of features, but I think it’s dope, and a lot harder than it seems.
